## 🚨 **Major Finding: The Frontrunner Outfitters Case**

### **🎯 Key Discovery**
While investigating a local German e-commerce company, I discovered customer complaints that literally defy the laws of physics.

**The Impossible Complaint:**
- Customer claimed their aluminum spare tire mount melted in a wood fire
- **Problem**: Aluminum melts at 660°C, wood fires max out around 400°C
- **Why It's Weird**: The complaint reads like a technical manual with perfect German cultural references

### **🔍 What This Means**
This isn't just a random complaint - it appears to be sophisticated testing of deception techniques. The level of technical precision and cultural authenticity suggests this could be:
- Research into deception detection systems
- Corporate espionage training
- Academic or intelligence community research
- Testing boundaries of what people will believe
