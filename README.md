## 🚨 **The Frontrunner Outfitters Case**

This one's weird. Started as a simple security check for a local business, but then...

### **What I Thought I'd Find**
- Basic security holes
- Outdated software
- Maybe some weak passwords

### **What I Actually Found**
- A legit company that got bought by a huge Swedish corporation
- Some customer complaints that literally defy the laws of physics
- Way too much technical detail in casual customer reviews
- Patterns that suggest something more sophisticated than typical customers

### **The Impossible Complaint**
My favorite part: someone claimed their aluminum spare tire mount melted in a wood fire. 
- Problem: Aluminum melts at 660°C
- Wood fires max out around 400°C
- Customer used perfect German technical terms
- Complaint was way too detailed and professional

Like, who writes a technical manual disguised as an angry customer review? It's fascinating!
